Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

I like peanuts and I like jelly, this sandwich has both and it is a great flavor combination.

  • Peanut Butter
  • Grape Jelly
  • Loaf of Sliced Bread
  1. Remove tie from sliced bread bag.
  2. Retrieve 2 pieces of slice bread from open bag.
  3. Replace tie on sliced bread bag.
  4. Open jars of peanut butter and grape jelly.
  5. Insert butter knife into peanut butter jar and spread the collected peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  6. Rinse butter knife clean.
  7. Insert butter knife into grape jelly jar and spread the collected grape jelly on the other slice of bread.
  8. Close jars of peanut butter and grape jelly.
  9. Place pieces of bread atop one another with the bare sides outward.
  10. Enjoy.

You will DIE if you have peanut allergies and eat this.
